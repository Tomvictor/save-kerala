About Us

We are a group of young engineers from Kochi, decided to do someting for the fellow brothers and sisters who where stranded by the devestating flood in Krala. Then we think ablout all possible solutions and came up with and idea of realtime web portal for instant help. At the time we starated coding we were only there individuals, But now are a large community of like minded peoples with lot of energy. It took us one night to build the entire project, It took only hours to spread the information and to populating with help requests. We all are doing our best to help people, those who suffers horribly these days. We congragulate everone with helping hearts, those were the part of the savekerala.xyz.
